Vaccines

Vaccine options & Intended use

DPT Vaccine

This vaccine protects against diphtheria, pertussis (whooping cough), and tetanus. It is given in a series of shots during infancy and childhood.

Read More

Hepatitis B Vaccine

Birth vaccination for hepatitis B is followed by a series of shots throughout infancy and childhood. It offers defence against hepatitis B, a liver-affecting viral infection.

Read More

HPV Vaccine

The HPV vaccine is recommended for preteens and teenagers to protect against several types of HPV that can cause cancers, including cervical cancer.

Read More

Travel Vaccines

Depending on travel destinations, adults may need vaccines like yellow fever, typhoid, cholera, and others to protect against diseases prevalent in specific regions.

Read More

Rabies Vaccine

Prevents rabies, a deadly viral infection that can be transmitted through the bite of an infected animal

Read More

COVID-19 Vaccines

Protection against the SARS-CoV-2 virus responsible for COVID-19.
